THE HISTORY OF THE MASIA

La Masía is an iconic place, full of history and tradition. Built in the 18th century, it has witnessed countless events that have shaped the local culture. From its beginnings as a farm to its transformation into a social gathering center, La Masía has maintained its essence throughout the years.

Visitors can explore its ancient rooms, enjoy the traditional architecture, and feel the atmosphere of times gone by. Today, La Masía is a symbol of cultural heritage, offering a glimpse into rural life of the past and serving as a meeting place for nature lovers.
