What is Masia Paradise?

This incredible traditional house is located in the heart of the Spanish forest, just 30 minutes from the center of Barcelona. Surrounded by lush greenery and the serene sounds of nature, it offers a peaceful retreat for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. Perfect for enjoying nature and disconnecting from daily stress, the house provides a unique blend of rustic charm and modern comfort.

Why us?

Our commitment to sustainability sets us apart, as we prioritize eco-friendly practices to preserve the natural beauty surrounding us. By choosing us, you're not just enjoying a unique getaway; you're also supporting responsible tourism. Join us in making a positive impact on the environment while you relax and rejuvenate!
